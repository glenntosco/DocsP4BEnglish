# Tax Authority

Here you will configure the tax authority â€” this is the entity to which you will remit your sales taxes.

**Master Data > Accounting > Tax Authority**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (18).png" alt="P4 Books Tax Authority list"><figcaption><p>Tax Authority List</p></figcaption></figure>

Once on the Tax Authority setup screen, click the New button.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (11).png" alt="P4 Books New Tax Authority form"><figcaption><p>New Tax Authority Form</p></figcaption></figure>

Here you will enter the name of the Tax Authority and select the G/L Account for this Tax Authority.

The typical setup is to enter the sales tax payable general ledger account.
